============================================================
  RANDY MEANEY WEBSITE — SETUP GUIDE
  (Step-by-step, no tech experience needed!)
============================================================

WHAT YOU NEED BEFORE STARTING:
  1. A free Netlify account  →  netlify.com  (sign up with email)
  2. A Stripe account        →  stripe.com   (sign up with email & bank info)

============================================================
STEP 1 — GET YOUR STRIPE KEYS
============================================================

1. Go to stripe.com and log in
2. Click "Developers" in the top menu
3. Click "API keys"
4. Copy the "Publishable key" (starts with pk_live_...)
5. Copy the "Secret key" (starts with sk_live_...)
   → Keep these safe, like a password!

============================================================
STEP 2 — DEPLOY TO NETLIFY
============================================================

1. Go to netlify.com and log in
2. Click "Add new site" → "Deploy manually"
3. Drag the entire "randy-meaney-website" FOLDER onto the page
4. Wait a moment — your site is now live!
5. Netlify gives you a free link like: https://random-name.netlify.app
   (You can rename it in Site Settings → Change site name)

============================================================
STEP 3 — SET UP YOUR SECRET SETTINGS
============================================================

In Netlify, go to:
  Site Settings → Environment Variables → Add a variable

Add these THREE variables:

  Variable name: ADMIN_PASSWORD
  Value: (choose your own password — something easy to remember!)

  Variable name: STRIPE_SECRET_KEY
  Value: (paste your Stripe secret key from Step 1)

  Variable name: STRIPE_PUBLISHABLE_KEY
  Value: (paste your Stripe publishable key from Step 1)

After adding all three, click "Deploy site" to restart it.

============================================================
STEP 4 — MANAGING YOUR SHOP
============================================================

To add/edit your jewelry items:
1. Go to YourSite.netlify.app/admin
2. Enter your ADMIN_PASSWORD
3. You'll see 12 slots — fill in as many as you like:
   - Click the photo area to upload a picture
   - Type the item name
   - Enter the price
   - Add a short description (optional)
4. Click "Save All Changes" when done
5. Your site updates right away!

============================================================
STEP 5 — SHARE YOUR LINK
============================================================

Copy your Netlify link (e.g. https://randymeaney.netlify.app)
and paste it into your Instagram bio or Facebook profile!

============================================================
CUSTOM DOMAIN (OPTIONAL)
============================================================

If you want a custom address like randymeaney.com (~$12/year):
1. Buy a domain at namecheap.com or godaddy.com
2. In Netlify: Site Settings → Domain Management → Add domain
3. Follow Netlify's instructions to connect it
